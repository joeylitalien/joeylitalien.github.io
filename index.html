Bonjour, Hello!

I am a Ph.D. student at the McGill Graphics & Imaging Lab (GIL), working under Derek Nowrouzezahrai. My main research interests lie at the intersection of physically-based rendering and machine learning. More precisely, I am interested in applying deep learning techniques to improve current physically-based rendering algorithms (e.g., denoising, path guiding, sampling). I also have an interest for differentiable rendering and Markov chain Monte Carlo methods for light transport simulations.

If you'd like to chat, feel free to send me an email.
